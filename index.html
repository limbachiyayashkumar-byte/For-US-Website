How it works

Three small things, done well

For Us isn't trying to be everything. It does a few things that actually help two people spend better time together.

SPIN

One tap, one idea

Can't decide what to do tonight? Spin the wheel and let it choose for you — the deceleration and little fanfare make even a slow Tuesday feel like an event.

KEEP

A record of "us"

Every idea you've spun and tried gets kept close by, so you can look back on the growing list of things you've done together.

LOCAL

Made to just work

No account to create, no feed to scroll. For Us is built to open fast, do its job, and get out of the way of your evening.
