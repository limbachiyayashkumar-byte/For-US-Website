Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026
This policy is a starting point written for how For Us works today — a local, account-free app with ad support. Before publishing, read it against what the app actually does and adjust anything that doesn't match (for example, if you add analytics, cloud sync, or an account system later).

For Us ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by an independent developer based in India. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and who it's shared with when you use For Us on Android.

Information we collect

For Us is designed to work without an account. The app does not ask for your name, email, or phone number, and does not require you to sign in to use its core features.

  • Activity data you create (such as the list of date ideas or activities you've saved or marked as done) is stored locally on your device, not on our servers.
  • Advertising identifiers and usage data may be collected automatically by our advertising partner, Google AdMob, as described below.

Advertising

For Us shows ads through Google AdMob to keep the app free. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), general device information, and interaction data to serve and measure ads, including personalised ads where permitted by your region and settings.

Google's use of this data is governed by Google's own privacy policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy. You can control ad personalisation through your device's Google settings at any time.

Our app-ads.txt declaration, required by Google for authorised ad sellers, is published at /app-ads.txt.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect or store your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not access your contacts, messages, or photos.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data storage and security

Content you create inside the app is stored locally on your device. If a future version of the app adds cloud backup or sync, this policy will be updated to describe what's stored, where, and for how long before that feature ships.

Children's privacy

For Us is intended for couples and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by your local law). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Third-party services

Aside from Google AdMob, For Us does not currently integrate third-party analytics or authentication services. If that changes, this section will be updated to name the service and what it collects.

Your choices

  • You can reset or clear the app's local data at any time from your device's app settings.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising through your device's Google Ads settings.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time, which removes all locally stored data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or how your data is handled? Reach out at contact@glitgrace.in — see the Contact page for details.
